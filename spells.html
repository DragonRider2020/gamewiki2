the spells are obtained out of dungeons and places. each one has an keyboard combination to use it. the button A closes and opens the spell list. these are the spell locations and letters:
  • shatter: S. obtained from the mayor.
  • freeze: F. obtained from the temple of lake tremora.
  • gust: G. obtained from skymountain peak.
  • rock: R. obtained from the ground maze.
  • flame: FL. obtained form the fire tomb.
