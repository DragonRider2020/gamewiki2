the boss battle

drakin shoots three fireballs wich can be gust over. after 1-2
shots he summons 10 shots of red thunder that appear to random positions.
to damage drakin, the player must look at the eye on his chest, wich
changes color to blue, red, white or green. the spell to attack
is determined by the color of the eye:
  • red: flame spell
  • blue: freeze spell
  • green: rock spell
  • white: gust spell (hit the eye)
he has 16 lives in total and a 1 sec of invulnerability after damage.
